Our Goal

At No More Caps Foundation, we are dedicated to restore and give to anyone who wants their hair looking amazing. They will no longer have to wear hoodies or hats to hide their haircut and feel insecure. We also do hair transplants for people who aren't capable of growing hair or is fighting an illness. Everyone here at No More Caps Foundation believes no one should have to go through this, so we want to change their life forever but we can't do that without the help of the people.

How You Can Help.

Every weekday, we set up a haircut stand to give the people free haircuts or hair transplants. Every donation wil go towards the things needed to bless these people. Down below is where you can donate if you want, it will be much appreciated.
